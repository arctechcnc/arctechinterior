About Us

Our Legacy: A 50-Year Journey of Excellence

🏛️

The Foundation (1970s): My Construction Co

The story of our expertise began in 1970, when two brothers united their passion to form "My Construction Co". Together, they built a reputation for grit and structural integrity, laying the first bricks of what would become a half-century legacy in Mumbai’s construction industry.

🏗️

The Era of Specialization (2000): Indian Construction Co.

Following the passing of the elder brother, the younger brother carried the vision forward by founding Indian Construction Co. (ICC) in 2000. Under his leadership, the firm became a prestigious Registered Contractor with MHADA. For years, ICC successfully executed landmark projects for MHADA, Indian Railways, and the MES, earning official completion certificates that define our commitment to quality.

📈

The Strategic Evolution (2009 – 2012)

By 2009, the landscape of Mumbai’s real estate shifted as MHADA reconstruction projects underwent significant policy changes. Recognizing the need to adapt to a changing market and the demand for specialized design, the next chapter began.

🚀

The Modern Vision (2012 – Present): ArcTech Interior LLP

In 2012, Er. Shakil Ahmed Behlim (son of the younger brother and a DCE and B.Tech Civil Engineer) established ArcTech Interior. Bringing over 20 years of on-site wisdom and a modern engineering perspective, he transitioned the firm into an LLP in 2019.

Today, ArcTech Interior LLP bridges the gap between decades of traditional experience and cutting-edge technology:

  • Turnkey Solutions: Seamlessly managing Construction and Interior projects from start to finish.
  • Structural Experts: Specializing in the Repair and Maintenance of RCC buildings.
  • Advanced Manufacturing: We have integrated In-house CNC Machining to provide our clients with precision-engineered architectural elements and world-class finishes.
